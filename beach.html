Wahoo Beach Activities

Welcome to Wahoo Beach! Just steps away from Wahoo Island Resort and filled with activities, you're sure to have the time of your life.

  • Aloha Boat Tours are an unforgettable way to explore the bay. With six boats in their fleet, Aloha can charter anything from pleasure cruises, a whale watching tours, SCUBA dive and snorkeling trips, and beyond.
  • Tim's Surf Lessons are the way to go if you don't want to leave without catching your first wave. Tim has five additional instructors, ensuring that each person gets plenty of instruction throughout their sessions.
  • Sky High Helecopter Tours are a fantastic way to see the island from a bird's eye view. Staring from the South shore, you'll work your way around the coastline to the East side of the island, then you'll be swept across 72,491 acres of dense forest.
