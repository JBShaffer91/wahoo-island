Explore Wahoo

Ready for the adventure of a lifetime? You've come to the right island! From hiking to off road tours, you'll go home with an epic story.

  • Andy's Off Road Tours offers Jeep and ATV tours with multiple packages available. Destinations include a short, round trip tour through a beautiful fruit plantation, a mid-length trip to the hard-to-reach Paradise Falls for a complementary lunch, and a day trip around the entire island, stopping off at some of the best food trucks on the island!
  • Ma'halo Guides will guide you through dense jungle to some of the most beautiful destinations on the island, and all on foot and by kayak! Hike and/or paddle your way to memories that will last a lifetime with some of the island's most experienced guides. Make it even more interesting by tossing your shoes in your pack and going local style (barefoot). No matter which destination you choose, a free lunch is included in every adventure.
  • Ke'he Zipline will take you through the jungle in style! With a series of ziplines spanning 2,400 feet across a legendary landscape, this should be on everyone's to-do list.
