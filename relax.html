Ho'omaha Spa

Escape a sanctuary of relaxation and renewal. Let our world class treatments pamper you in the island spirit of aloha. At Ho'omaha Spa, the natural landscape of Wahoo is woven harmoniously with sophisticated modern design. You'll find a luxurious pre-treatment suite, men's and women's locker rooms, private treatment rooms, and a retail boutique. Planning an experience for two? Fall in love with our couple's treatment rooms, complete with a private lanai, rainfall shower heads, and the island's only Japanese Ofuro soaking tub. You'll be swept away to pure paradise.

Wahoo Shopping

Wahoo is a wonderful place for anyone who loves to shop. The downtown area has some of the most unique, independently owned shops on the island - and it's entirely walkable! With shops offering local style by local designers, to some of the most unique, handmade souvenirs you can imagine. There are plenty of eateries along the way, too!
